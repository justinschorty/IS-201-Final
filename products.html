First you can SEARCH

The first thing to do when you want to search for yourself to try and find truth in something is to DO THE WORK! When we put forth the effort to read even a couple verses of the word of God we are showing our desire to understand God's will fo rus and we allow ourselves the opportunity to learn new things.

Then you can PONDER

Reading alone is a great way for us to come closer to Christ but when we add pondering and studying to our experience we open ourselves up to a far richer learning experience. The method of study is up to you but when we write out our thoughts or take a deep dive into words or the meaning of verses, we can extract more meaningful messages from the words of the prophets and apostles and the stories become much more real and personal to us.

And Finally You Can PRAY

Finally when we pray we are following the prophet Moroni's council (Moroni 10:3-5) and we can ask God if what we have read and studied is true! When we read the scriptures God speaks to us, and when we pray we are able to speak to God! If you have any questions or don't know how to pray, find your local missioniaries or visit comeuntochrist.org.
